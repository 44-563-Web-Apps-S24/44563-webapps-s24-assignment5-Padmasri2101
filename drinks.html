When reducing the size of the browser window slowly, you will notice that the row containing the drink names breaks first before the row containing the letters. This happens because the letters in the first row are single characters, which can easily stack vertically as the window size decreases. However, the drink names in the second row are longer and require more horizontal space. As the window width decreases, the longer drink names cannot fit within their respective columns, causing them to wrap onto the next line, thus breaking the row. Therefore, the drink names break first due to their length and the limited horizontal space available.
